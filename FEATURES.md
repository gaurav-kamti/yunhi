## üéµ 3-Stage Music System

Different music for each stage of the experience.

### Stage 1: Before Birthday
- Plays during the countdown
- Creates anticipation
- Loops continuously

### Stage 2: Birthday Moment
- Plays when countdown reaches zero
- Special celebration music
- Plays for 30 seconds

### Stage 3: Gift Page
- Background music for the gift experience
- Loops throughout all 6 gift stages
- Creates emotional atmosphere

**Supported formats:** MP3, WAV, OGG  
**Recommended:** MP3 for best compatibility  
**File size:** Under 10MB per file

## üíå Guestbook System

Collect birthday wishes from friends.

**Features:**
- 15-word limit per message
- Real-time word counter
- Stored in browser localStorage
- Appears in gift page Stage 2
- No server required

**How it works:**
1. Friends visit the countdown page
2. Fill in name and message
3. Submit wish
4. Message saved locally
5. Appears in gift page automatically
